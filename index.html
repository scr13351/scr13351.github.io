Bexley Arthur Rogers: A Complex Canine

It is important to know that Bexley, my dog, is a beautiful English Labrador who likes loves to:

  • lick
  • play
  • and most of all EAT!
A Schedule of Bexley's Day:
MorningAfternoonNight
Wake up for his first mealBeg for treatsBeg for his last meal
First Nap around 10:00 am in the sunGo over to the neighbor's housePlay with his toys after dinner
Wait by the fridge for a carrotEat all of the neighbor's cat foodOne more nap before bed time

While looking at the table above, you may have notice the frequency at which Bexley likes to eat, think about eating, and beg for more food even after he has eaten. He is a handsome dog for sure, but he is no stranger to struggling with his weight. He is only 3 years old yet many people consider him an old dog due to his weight. When he was just 1 He weighed an upwards of 75 pounds. Do you have a Labrador, and are you wondering if he is too fat? Here is a helpful website to answer any questions you may have about your dog's personal weight: Fat-Labrador
